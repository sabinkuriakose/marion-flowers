Our Story

Crafting Beauty
for Every Moment

Marion Flowers & Decor is Vytilla's trusted name in floral artistry. Nestled in the heart of Ambelipadam, Ernakulam, we bring colour, fragrance, and joy to your most precious moments — from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, with the freshest blooms sourced daily.

🌸

Daily Fresh Flowers

We source the freshest blooms every morning so your arrangements always look and smell their absolute best.

🎨

Custom Designs

Every order is crafted to match your vision, theme, and budget — no two arrangements are ever alike.

🚚

On-Time Delivery

Reliable delivery and on-site setup across Ernakulam, ensuring everything is perfect before your event begins.

What We Offer

Our Floral Services

From intimate bouquets to large-scale event transformations, we offer a complete range of floral and decoration services tailored to your every need.

💐 01

Bouquets & Arrangements

Hand-crafted bouquets for birthdays, anniversaries, proposals, gifting, and every special moment in between. Custom styles — round, cascade, hand-tied, and more.

🚗 02

Car Decoration

Elegant floral car decoration for weddings, receptions, and VIP events. Fresh flowers and ribbons, beautifully arranged to make every grand arrival memorable.

🕊️ 03

Cemetery & Memorial Decoration

Respectful and heartfelt floral tributes for funerals, remembrance days, and cemetery occasions. Wreaths, crosses, and custom memorial arrangements.

🏢 04

Institutional Decoration

Beautify offices, hospitals, hotels, schools, and corporate spaces with regular floral arrangements or one-time event setups that leave a lasting impression.

💒 05

Wedding Decoration

Complete wedding floral services — stage decoration, mandap florals, aisle arrangements, entrance arches, table centrepieces, and bridal bouquets.

🎉 06

Event & Stage Decoration

Birthdays, receptions, corporate launches, cultural events — we transform any stage or venue into a stunning floral spectacle, large or intimate.

🏠 07

Home & Pooja Decoration

Floral decoration for housewarming, Vishu, Onam, Christmas, and all festive occasions. Bring the fragrance and colour of fresh flowers into your home.

🌿 08

Potted Plants & Gifting

A curated selection of indoor plants, terrariums, and gift hampers — perfect for corporate gifting, housewarmings, and anyone who loves greenery.
